About

The mission of the littleTiger site is to provide maximum pointlessness with the minimum of financial outlay and intellectual effort.

The name was chosen by our son, Jonathon, but has nothing to do with animals and a lot to do with cutlery. I'll tell you when you're old enough...

The entire website has been carefully hand-crafted to look its best when viewed on our web browser at home. No tigers, little or otherwise, were harmed in the development of these pages.
